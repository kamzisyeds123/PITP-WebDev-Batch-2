About TCF School

The Citizens Foundation (TCF) is a professionally managed, non-profit organisation set up in 1995 by a group of citizens who wanted to bring about positive social change through education. 30 years later, TCF is now one of Pakistan’s leading organisations in the field of education for the less privileged.

Our Mission

Quality Education Through the power of quality education, enabling moral, spiritual and intellectual enlightenment.

Our Vision

Positive Change To remove barriers of class and privilege to make the citizens of Pakistan agents of positive change.
